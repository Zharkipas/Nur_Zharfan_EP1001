PCB Fabrication

On this page i have recorded my process of fabricating my several PCBs

The preparation

Before fabricating any PCBs i need to start by getting the files ready

For my "Hello World" board i had to export the PCB design from Eagle while i obtained the PCB designs for my FTDI and UPDI boards from the elective website

As for the "Hello World" I had to export 2 different files for fabrication. The first file is a png file of the traces of the board while the second is a png file of the outline of the board

Next, I imported the png images into the mods software found on the Further Digital Fabrication website

With mods, i exported the gcode to CNC route the board onto a thumbdrive and proceeded to the stepcraft machine to fabricate the boards

The machining

At the stepcraft machine, I uploaded the gcode for the traces of the chosen board first and attached a 30 degree cutter to the machine

I then had to re-zero the machine and do a air cut to ensure that there were no issues with the program. Once all check were done, I finally started cutting the copper board. After the maching of the traces is completed, i changed out the cutter for a flat endmill to cut out the outline of the board

I repeated this process for all 3 boards

Lastly, I soldered all of the electronics onto the boards once i finished routing all 3 boards
