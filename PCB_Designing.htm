PCB Designing

On this page i have recorded my process of designing and exporting my "Hello World" PCB design

Software used

I had chosen to use the Eagle sofware by Autodesk as i found it the easiest to use in terms of designing the pcb and also importing different libraries needed for my design

Here is the link to the Eagle software:*insert link

Steps taken

I first started by doing the schematic diagram of the desired pcb. This diagram determines how each component is connected to one another

insert image

Next, I worked on the board design, after switching to the board view i rearranged the components to allow fo the most compact design as to minismise the wastage of materials

insert image

I had to also ensure that my line thickness was correct and that the corners were not too sharp which could be an issue when machining

After finalising the design i ran a checking function in Eagle to ensure that the design had no errors
