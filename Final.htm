The Keypad

On this page i have recorded my process of designing and fabricating my keypad

Designing

I first started with the PCB design and schemetics as to know how my different electronics will link to one another. Once I had the final PCB design, I took its dimensions and started designing the housing for my keypad

Fabrication

Once done with the designs i moved on to fabricating my PCB and keypad housing. I used a variety of methods such as large and small scale CNC and 3D printing

Assembly

With the completion of the fabrication, it was time to put all the parts together. First was soldering the electronics to the PCB i routed and testing that all the parts were not faulty. Next i fitted the switches and PCB into the housing to check that the sizing was correct. Thankfully the dimensions were just nice

Programming

Now that ive checked for faulty electronics and i know that the individual parts were of the right size, I could start programming my PCB to be a key pad. I utilized a similiar code to my programming assignment earlier in the module to communicate the button pressees on my PCB to my PC to simulate different keyboard presses
