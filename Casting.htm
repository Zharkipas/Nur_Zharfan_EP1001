Casting and Molding

On this page i have recorded my process of Casting and Molding

The design

I first started with a 3D design of the product i wanted which would be used to produce a reusable mold of the product

The Mold and Casting

I started by exporting the gcode to machine out the 3D design using the stepcraft machine. I then prepared the stepcraft machine and the block of styrofoam we would be machining before starting the machining process

Afterwards, I poured a 2 part silicone mixture into the machined piece and waited 24hrs for the mixture to cure. After the 24hrs, the silicone part was ready to be used as a reusable mold to cast with

Next, it was time to pour the 2 part resin mixture to cast the desired product. With the chosen mixture it only took a couple of minutes for it to cure.
