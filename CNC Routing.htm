CNC Routing

On this page i have recorded my process of designing and fabricating my table side shelf

The design

Before being able to fabricate a product i had to design it first. I used Fusion 360 for this process

Since i was using the Large scale cnc router i had to compensate for the 6mm diameter cutter being used to cut my pieces therefore i used a dogbone library found EP1001 website to auto add the additional feature in my design

The Fabrication

Before I started cutting, there were a few things to do. I first had to export my 3D model as 2D faces in a DXF file format, this was simple as fusion allows us to export the different faces on the model as DXF files and all i had to do was comile them into 1 DXF file

Next, I had to choose the plywood with the right thickness and screw it down onto the sacrificial board of the CNC machine. Only then did i import my DXF file into VcarvePro onto the machine's computer

Afterwards, i programmed the different toolpaths, rezeroed the machine and ensured that all of the settings were in order before starting the machine.

After machining the indicidual pieces, i then removed them from the board and sanded them down to remove excess fibres from the piece to have a smoother finish on the pieces. All that was left was to assemble
